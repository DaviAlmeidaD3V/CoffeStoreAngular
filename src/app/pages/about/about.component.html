Alta Morgiana

known as produce a The best world cafes

Known worldwide for the production of high quality coffees, Alta Mogiana, located on the border between Minas Gerais and São Paulo have cultivated coffee for over 200 years.

What makes this region so special is its altitude, which varies between 900 and 1000 meters, its soil rich in nutrients and its mild temperature, which in addition to favoring the cultivation of noble grains, allows for better fruit maturation, the that determines the quality and complex flavors of coffee.

Better than being 100% Arabica, is to be 100% coffee.

Being 100% coffee is the guarantee of consuming the best of the drink, free of impurities and with selected beans.

The Arabica bean from the Alta Mogiana region is considered by specialists to be one of the best coffees in the world.

In addition, iá café has selection, roasting and grinding processes as noble as its origin, following the legacy of a coffee family.
